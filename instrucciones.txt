How to win:
In order to win, you must achieve a score of 50 points

Types of apples:
    - red: if eaten, score goes up by one
    - purple: if eaten, snake dies
    - blue: if eaten, snake moves slower for three turns
    - gold: if eaten, score goes up by three
    - pink: if eaten, a purple apple dissapears

In case of touching the screen edges, snake dies.
